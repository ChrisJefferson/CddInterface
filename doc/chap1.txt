  [1X1.1 [33X[0;0YWhy CddInterface[133X[101X
  
  [33X[0;0YWe  know  that  every  convex polyhedron has two representations, one as the
  intersection  of  finite  halfspaces  and  the other as Minkowski sum of the
  convex  hull of finite points and the nonnegative hull of finite directions.
  These   are  called  [23XH[123X-representation  and  [23XV[123X-representation,  respectively.
  [23X\newline[123X  CddInterface  is  a gap interface to the C package [23X\textbf{cddlib}[123X
  which among other things can translate between these two representations.[133X
  
  
  [1X1.2 [33X[0;0YH-representation and V-representation of polyhedra[133X[101X
  
  [33X[0;0YLet us start by introducing the [23XH[123X-representation. Let [23XA[123X be [23Xm \times d[123X matrix
  and  let  [23Xb[123X  be  a  column  [23Xm[123X-vector. The [23XH[123X-representation of the polyhedron
  defined  by  the  system  [23Xb+Ax  \geq  0[123X of [23Xm[123X inequalities and [23Xd[123X variables [23Xx=
  (x_1,\dots,x_d)[123X  is as follows: [23X \newline \textbf{H-representation}[123X [23X\newline
  \textbf{linearity}\;t,\;\;[i_1,   i_2,  \dots,i_t][123X  [23X\newline  \textbf{begin}[123X
  [23X\newline  m  \times  (d+1)[123X  numbertype  [23X\newline  b \;\;\;\;\;\;\;\;\;\;\; A[123X
  [23X\newline  \textbf{end}[123X  [23X\newline[123X The linearity line is added when we want to
  specify  that  some  rows  of  the system [23Xb+Ax[123X are equalities. That is, [23Xk\in
  \{i_1, i_2, \dots,i_t\}[123X means that the row [23Xk[123X of the system [23Xb+Ax[123X is specified
  to be equality.[133X
  
  [33X[0;0YFor example, the [23XH[123X-representation of the polyhedron defined by the following
  system:[133X
  
  
  [24X[33X[0;6Y\newline  4-3x_1+6x_2-5x_4  =  0, \newline 1+2x_1-2x_2-7x_3 \geq 0, \newline
  -3x_2+5x_4 = 0\newline[133X

  [33X[0;0Yis    the    following:    [23X\newline    \textbf{H-representation}[123X    [23X\newline
  \textbf{linearity}\;2,\;\;[1, 3][123X [23X\newline \textbf{begin}[123X [23X\newline 3 \times 5[123X
  rational  [23X\newline 4\;\;\;-3\;\;\;\;\;\;\;\;\;6\;\;\;\;\;\;\;\;\;0\;\;\;\;-5[123X
  [23X\newline  1\;\;\;\;\;\;\;\;2\;\;\;\;-2\;\;\;\;-7\;\;\;\;\;\;\;\;\;0[123X [23X\newline
  0\;\;\;\;\;\;\;\;0\;\;\;\;-3\;\;\;\;\;\;\;\;\;0\;\;\;\;\;\;\;\;\;5[123X  [23X\newline
  \textbf{end}[123X [23X\newline \newline[123X[133X
  
  [33X[0;0YNext we define Polyhedra [23XV[123X-format. Let [23XP[123X be represented by [23Xn[123X gerating points
  and [23Xs[123X generating directions (rays) as[133X
  
  
  [24X[33X[0;6YP  =  \mathrm{conv}(v_1  , \dots , v_n ) + \mathrm{nonneg}(r_{n+1} , \dots ,
  r_{n+s} ).[133X

  [33X[0;0YThen the Polyhedra [23XV[123X-format is for [23XP[123X is:[133X
  
  [33X[0;0Y[23X\newline         \newline         \textbf{V-representation}[123X         [23X\newline

  [23X\newline     \vdots    \;\;\;\;\;\;\;\;\;\;\;\;\;    \vdots[123X    [23X\newline    1

  [23X\newline     \vdots    \;\;\;\;\;\;\;\;\;\;\;\;\;    \vdots[123X    [23X\newline    0

  [33X[0;0YIn  the  above  format  the generating points and generating rays may appear

  \{i_1  , i_2 , . . . , i_t \}[123X specifies that the [23Xk[123X-th generator is specified

  longer  nonnegative  but still the coefficients for all [23Xv_i[123Xâ€™s must sum up to
  one. [23X\newline[123X[133X
