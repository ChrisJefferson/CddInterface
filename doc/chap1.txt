  let  [23Xb[123X  be a column [23Xm[123X-vector. The H-representation of the polyhedron defined
  by   the   system  [23Xb+Ax  \geq  0[123X  of  [23Xm[123X  inequalities  and  [23Xd[123X  variables  [23Xx=
  (x_1,\dots,x_d)[123X  is  as follows: [23X\newline \newline \textbf{H-representation}[123X
  [23X\newline    \textbf{linearity}\;t,\;\;[i_1,    i_2,    \dots,i_t][123X   [23X\newline
  \textbf{begin}[123X    [23X\newline    m   \times   (d+1)[123X   numbertype   [23X\newline   b
  \;\;\;\;\;\;\;\;\;\;\;  A[123X  [23X\newline \textbf{end}[123X [23X\newline[123X The linearity line
  is  added  when  we  want  to  specify that some rows of the system [23Xb+Ax[123X are

  the   system   [23Xb+Ax[123X   is   specified   to  be  equality.  For  example,  the
  H-representation of the polyhedron defined by the following system: [23X\newline
  \newline  4-3x_1+6x_2-5x_4  =  0[123X  [23X\newline  1+2x_1-2x_2-7x_3 \geq 0[123X [23X\newline
  -3x_2+5x_4     =     0\newline\newline[123X     is     as    follows:    [23X\newline
  \textbf{H-representation}[123X [23X\newline \textbf{linearity}\;2,\;\;[1, 3][123X [23X\newline
  \textbf{begin}[123X     [23X\newline     3     \times     5[123X     rational     [23X\newline
